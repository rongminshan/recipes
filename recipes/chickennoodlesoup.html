Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This soup is perfect for those who want a healthier option of the store brand chicken noodle soups. This recipe serves 6.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 4 cans chicken broth (14.5oz)
  • 1 can vegetable broth (14.5oz)
  • 1/2 lb chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1.5 cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Melt butter ina large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and celery and cook unil just tender, or 5 minutes.
  2. Add the chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
