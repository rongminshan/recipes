Italian Wedding Soup

Description

A delicious soup where you can make your own meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tbs dry bread crumbs
  • 1 tbs grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 5/75 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups thinly sliced escarole
  • 1 cup uncooked orzo pasta
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped carrot

Instructions

  1. Combine beef, egg, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, basil, and onion powder in a bowl. Shape into 3/4 inch balls.
  2. Heat broth in a large pot over medium-high heat until boiling. Stir in escarole, orzo, carrot, and meatballs and return to boil. Reduce heat to medium and cook at a slow boil, stirring frequently to prevent sticking, until pasta is tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 minutes.
